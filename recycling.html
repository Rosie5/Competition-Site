Why not donate your time to recycling in your community?

Opportunities to volunteer in Cleaning and Greening! Get your hands dirty and help your local area at the same time! Ever wanted to say thanks for the amazing festival you went to this summer? Or want to help restore a local park, forest, or pond to its natural beauty? If all of that appeals to you then why not volunteer to get involved in recycling in your local area?


Take Part in 3 Easy Steps

  1. Search for a scheme and register your interest
  2. Wait 24 hours to be contacted via phone or email
  3. Attend your befriending induction and get started!
