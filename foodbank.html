BonVol Food Bank Services

Why not donate your time to food banks in your community?
We're seeking new volunteers now!

There are approximately 13 million people in the UK living below the poverty line. Volunteering for a food bank, gives you the opportunity to provide support to many families every day. Most food bank volunteers help sort donated food products, separate items into food categories, check expiration dates, discard damaged product and check for recalled items.

Take Part in 3 Easy Steps

  1. Search for a scheme and register your interest
  2. Wait 24 hours to be contacted via phone or email
  3. Attend your food bank induction and get started!
