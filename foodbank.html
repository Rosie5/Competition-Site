Food Banks

Why?

There are approximately 13 million people in the UK living below the poverty line. Volunteering for a food bank, gives you the opportunity to provide support to many families every day.

What?

Most food bank volunteers help sort donated food products, separate items into food categories, check expiration dates, discard damaged product and check for recalled items.

How?
